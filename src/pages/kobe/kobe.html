The Mamba Mentality Lives On Forever

You showed us what hard-work, mental toughness, and discipline looks like not only on the court, but also off of it. You showed us how important it is to pick yourself up after a setback, and the importance of approaching any challenge with focus and determination. You showed us that it's ok to revinvent yourself. You showed us what it looks like to adapt to the situation that is in front of you. To be in the present because that's what is truly important. You showed us how to weather a storm and come out a better player and person.

You shined a light on the responsibility of moving the game forward and teaching the next generation to focus on the fundamentals. If you don't play sports, then that's ok because you can always try and be the best version of yourself. That's the true definition of the Mamba Mentality. Thank you for leaving us a blueprint. Thank you for the Mamba Mentality. May your eternal soul forever rest in peace. If you want to read more about Kobe Bryant's life, you can read his Wikipedia Page.
